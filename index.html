Who We Are

Nexus T&D Solutions Private Limited is a specialist engineering consultancy dedicated to enhancing the reliability, safety, and performance of high-voltage electrical infrastructure.

We support utilities, EPC contractors, industries, and renewable developers throughout the complete lifecycle of AIS and GIS substations—from design verification and factory inspections to commissioning, maintenance, audits, and emergency restoration.
